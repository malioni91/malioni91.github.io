Intro

Alba-bot is an intelligent conversational agent that that assists travelers during their visit in Albania. She can provide recommendations for places to visit, check the weather for you, and also provide you with background information on the local history, places, etc.

Scenario

You have just arrived in Tirana, Albania and are trying to plan what to do in the next couple of days that you will be in the country.

Instructions

Pre-requisite: You need to have and be logged into your personal Facebook account. 1. Go to this page. 2. Click the messenger icon on the right side of the screen(if browsing from desktop/laptop:

Tasks

  1. Introduce yourself to Alba bot, and have a short dialog.
  2. You want to know if it's going to rain or not in a particular city in Albania. Ask the bot for the answer.
  3. You want to know if there are any good restaurants in the city of your choice. Ask the bot for suggestions.
  4. You like visiting museums around the country. Ask the bot for suggestions regarding museums.
  5. You are looking for a place to stay in town. Ask Alba bot to suggest you some hotels in the area.
  6. After interacting for a while with Alba-bot, please fill in this survey.
